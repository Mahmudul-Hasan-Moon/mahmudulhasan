About Me

Let's look at my details

Hello, I am Mahmudul Hasan Moon. A University Teacher, Researcher, Writer and Content Creator.

I am working on Data Science, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Remote Sensing Image Analysis, AI-based Application Development, Computer Security. Currently I am working with different team for Multidisciplinary Research.

Work Experiences

I am a moderator in one of the largest research learning platforms in Bangladesh named “Be Researcher BD (BRBD)” and I am the instructor of popular online free course named “Python for Research (Multidisciplinary)”. I also serve as an online instructor of Python and Data Science in the USA, Italy, and Denmark. I have a number of journals, conferences, and book chapters. In my YouTube channel, you will get different videos related to computer science academics and research tools.
